Know your Slytherin

Hello there! Welcome to the Slytherin.info. Here you can discover more about members of the House of the Serpent with a click of a button.
The House of the Serpent generally maintains its founder Salazar’s values, which are cunning, ambition and fraternity. Like Salazar, students in Slytherin House tend to value pure-blood heritage. Due to Slytherin’s association with pure-blood supremacy, the house does have a bit of a stigmatized reputation and students from the house are sometimes seen as self-serving. This is seen for example when Slytherin students flee during the Battle for Hogwarts choosing to save themselves instead of facing Lord Voldemort. However, not all Slytherins are bad, and the characteristics associated with the house can be used for good. Enough chit chat! Let’s get to know a bit more about some of our favourite Slytherins.
Click on any of the names on the lower left-hand side of this page and learn something about them. You may also choose to highlight the known pure-bloods or known half-bloods to make things easier!
